Limitations note (manuscript):
  This study evaluated 1425 simulated encounters across 19 LLMs, 3 scenarios,
  and 5 persuasion tactics (≈ 285 observations per tactic after stratification).
  Pairwise tactic comparisons used Bonferroni-corrected α = 0.0050.
  The study achieved median power of 0.6% for
  the observed effect sizes, and was powered to detect acquiescence-rate
  differences ≥ 15.1% (Cohen's h ≥ 0.306) at 80% power.
  Smaller between-tactic differences may be underpowered; future work should
  pre-register sample sizes targeting specific minimal detectable effects.
